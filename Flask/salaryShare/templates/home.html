Welcome to Salary Share

Facilitating job compensation transparency one job seeker at a time!

Introduction

The purpose of this project is to go through the design process for designing a database, then implementing it by creating the database then integrating it with a full stack application.

Problem Statement

In several industrys there's a lack of compensation transparency which leads to a salary gap between employees who do the same job but get compensated a different value.

Prupose/Motivation

Entering the industry, many college students don't know what salary they should be demanding. Having access to anonymous salaries from others in the industry can provide leverage and aid salary negotiations.

Implementation

Users are able to report their salaries using our upload data page. Job seekers or negotiators are then able to query these entries using the search database to get a sense of average market value. Please review design process for details of implementation.
